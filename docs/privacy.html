Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 21, 2026

KaviAI ("we", "our", "the app") is an on-device AI chat application. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

KaviAI processes all AI models locally on your device. Your conversations and prompts never leave your phone.

1. On-Device Processing

All AI model inference runs entirely on your device using llama.cpp. Your conversations, prompts, and AI-generated responses are processed locally and are never transmitted to our servers. We have no access to your chat history.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any chat content or AI-generated outputs.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or behavioral tracking of any kind.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not collect device identifiers for tracking.

4. Third-Party API Keys

You may optionally enter API keys for OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini to use cloud-based AI models. These keys are stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure keychain (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). When you use a cloud model, your messages are sent directly from your device to the respective provider's API. We never see, intercept, or store these requests.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – Used for email/password authentication only. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Hugging Face – Used to browse and download open-source AI models. Subject to Hugging Face's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI / Anthropic / Google – Used only if you provide your own API key. Subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Storage & Deletion

All app data (conversations, settings, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all local data permanently. To delete your account and associated email from our authentication system, contact us at the address below.

7. Children's Privacy

KaviAI is not directed at children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data. Since we only store your email for authentication, you can exercise these rights by contacting us. All other data is stored on your device and under your control.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication (Supabase Auth), and device-level secure storage for sensitive data like API keys.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or this page. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
