Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2026

KaviAI ("we", "our", "the app") is an on-device AI chat application. Your privacy is our highest priority. This policy explains what data we collect and how we handle it.

1. On-Device Processing

All AI model inference runs entirely on your device. Your conversations, prompts, and AI-generated responses are processed locally and are never transmitted to our servers.

2. Data We Collect

  • Email address – collected when you create an account, used solely for authentication and account management.
  • No conversation data – we do not collect, store, or transmit any chat content.
  • No analytics or tracking – we do not use any analytics SDKs or advertising trackers.

3. Third-Party API Keys

You may optionally enter API keys for OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google Gemini. These keys are stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure keychain. When you use a cloud model, your messages are sent directly from your device to the respective provider's API. We never see or store these requests.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – used for email/password authentication only. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Hugging Face – used to download open-source AI models. Subject to Hugging Face's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage

All app data (conversations, settings, downloaded models) is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all local data.

6. Children's Privacy

KaviAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the app or this page.

8. Contact
